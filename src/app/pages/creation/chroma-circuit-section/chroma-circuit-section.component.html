Chroma Circuit: Messenger's Chronicles

Chroma Circuit is a 2D platformer game I am currently creating. I am using GameMaker as a game engine and Aseprite for the creation of the characters and the environment.

The main character is called Neon and below is the spritesheet including the different frames of the character.

Neon character sheet.

This character sheet includes frames for:
the Idle animation (line 1),
the walk animation (line 3),
the jump animation (line 4),
the attack animation (line 5),
the death animation (line 6).
There is also alternatives faces for the Idle animation (line 2).

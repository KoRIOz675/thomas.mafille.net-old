LANubis

On Saturday, 8th of June 2024, ISEP Gaming Club, one of the associations I'm in, made the 14th version of their Gaming LAN. My job for this event was to create the Instagram posts to promote the event. Because the event was called LANubis (a contraction between the word LAN and the Egyptian god Anubis), the theme of the event was 'Egyptian'. So I created a background representing a dune with a night sky.

I made this image using Adobe Illustrator for the dune and Canva for the stars in the sky. Going from this, I made the version with the information on it. You can see the final version on the Instagram page of ISEP Gaming Club.
